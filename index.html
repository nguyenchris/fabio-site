About Chef Fabio Borges

Chef Fabio Borges loves the challenge to cook for amazing people who have made the lifestyle commitment to eat healthier. Born and raised in Brazil Chef Fabio was groomed to always use wholesome ingredients and creating dishes from scratch for an incredible delivery of Taste and Nutrition.

Moving to United States, Arizona in 2003 to work for the JW Marriott Desert Ridge Fabio had the opportunity to learn from the absolute best. With amazing mentors and cutting edge training at the time Fabio was able to start shifting menus with a more healthy-conscious flair especially when transferred to the island of Oahu, HI in 2007 to work at the JW Marriott Ihilani.

Chef Fabio began his full-time career as a Personal Chef/Fitness Coach in 2010 after deciding to leave his former position as Food & Beverage Manager/Director of Restaurants. Now after 10 years of joy in the kitchen, Chef Fabio works with professional athletes and families in Valley of the sun providing amazing personalized meal delivery services, in home private chef services and private catering.
